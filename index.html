Whitebox Learning / Prosthetics Engineering

Whitebox Learning provided us a simple yet powerful simulation tool that allowed us to practice building Prosthetics. After much prototyping, we built these models in the real world. Whitebox is "a complete Standards-Based STEM Learning System for Engineering, Science, and Technology Education classrooms, grades 6-12. Completely web-based, students can design, analyze, and simulate their designs, hundreds of times, from a web browser, and compete with other students throughout their district. Fully-Integrated Teacher LMS is included."
